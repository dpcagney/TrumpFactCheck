Why this exists

The goal

Not to tell you how to feel about any president — but to give you two things at once: accurate facts and historical perspective. Panic and apathy are both easy. Informed calm is harder, and better.

The sourcing

Every verdict links to non-partisan sources: the Associated Press, Reuters, PolitiFact, FactCheck.org, The Washington Post Fact Checker, court records, and government agencies. Every historical parallel is a documented event. Don't trust us — click the links.

The bet

That the country has metabolized demagogues, scandals, tariff fevers, and press-bashing before, and will again. The Constitution assumes leaders will be flawed and temporary — and routes around them. This too shall pass.
